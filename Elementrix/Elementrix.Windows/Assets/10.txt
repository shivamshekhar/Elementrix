Neon
Neon (Ne) is a colorless, non-metallic, very inert gas with an atomic number of ten. This member of the noble gas classification glows reddish orange in a vacuum tube.While experimenting on liquid air, Sir William Ramsay and Morris Travers discovered neon in 1898.
