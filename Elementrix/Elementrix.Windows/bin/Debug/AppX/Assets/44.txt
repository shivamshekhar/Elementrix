Ruthenium
Like the other metals of the platinum group, Ruthenium is inert to most other chemicals. Ruthenium produced is used for wear-resistant electrical contacts and the production of thick-film resistors. A minor application of Ruthenium is its use in some platinum alloys, and as a catalyst.
