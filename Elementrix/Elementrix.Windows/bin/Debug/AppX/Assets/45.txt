Rhodium
Rhodium is inert against corrosion and most aggressive chemicals, and because of its rarity, it is usually alloyed with Platinum or Palladium and applied in high-temperature and corrosion-resistive coatings. White gold is often plated with a thin Rhodium layer to improve its appearance.
